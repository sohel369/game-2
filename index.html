Legends of Integrity

Lionel Messi: In 2019, Messi admitted a penalty wasn’t a foul, sacrificing a scoring opportunity for fairness.

Paolo Di Canio: During a match, Di Canio halted play to aid an injured goalkeeper, forgoing a goal.

Ivan Fernandez Anaya: In a 2012 race, Anaya slowed down to correct a competitor’s early stop, ensuring a fair win.

Abbey D'Agostino & Nikki Hamblin: In the 2016 Olympics, after falling, they helped each other finish the 5,000m race, showcasing true sportsmanship.

Luz Long & Jesse Owens: In the 1936 Olympics, Long advised Owens on his long jump, aiding his rival’s qualification.
